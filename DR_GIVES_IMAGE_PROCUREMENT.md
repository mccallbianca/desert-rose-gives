## Priority tier 1 — Homepage and About page (launch-blocking)

These are the images grant reviewers will see first. Source these today.

### 1.1 Home hero — `home-hero.jpg` or `home-hero.webp`

- **Source:** Nappy.co (https://nappy.co) OR AllGo (https://allgo.com) — curated Black and body-diverse stock
- **Search query on Nappy.co:** `community gathering` or `family outdoor` or `neighborhood event`

## Compliance summary

**No AI for identity-representative imagery:** slots 1.1, 1.2a, 1.3, 2.3, 3.1, 3.2, 3.3, 4.1, 4.2, 4.3, 5.1, 5.3, 6.1, 6.3, 7.1, 7.2, 8.1, 8.3, 9.1.

**AI acceptable** (landscape, botanical, object, or generic professional scenes with no identity claims): slots 1.2b (professional team), 2.2 (desert rose), 2.4 (partners), 5.2 (phone closeup), 6.2 (landscape), 7.4 (setup shot), 8.2 (decorative SVG), 9.2 (notepad), 9.3 (landscape).

**Real photos required:** the five board portraits. No substitutes.

**Brand palette when selecting or generating:** royal blue + sky blue on near-black. Cool tones dominant. Avoid warm sunset, terracotta, pink, gold, or rose color stories — those conflict with the site's cold-clinical-cinematic aesthetic.

**Trauma-informed standard:** no images of distress, isolation framed as despair, or people in crisis. Every human image should show dignity, agency, or connection.

**Cashletic standard governing question:** does this look, sound, and feel unmistakably like Bianca? If it looks like generic corporate nonprofit stock, replace it.
