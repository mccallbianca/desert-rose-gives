# DR Gives — AEO/SEO Content Prompt Library

Every prompt below follows a four-part architecture:
**Context** (clinical/philosophical lens) → **Activity** (specific deliverable) →
**Personalization** (the blanks you fill in) → **Output Format** (exactly how
the result should be structured).

Paste any prompt into Claude, add your fill-ins, and push the resulting content
straight into a new blog post, program description, or social caption. All
prompts assume the reader/search engine/LLM will encounter this content on
desertrosegives.org.

---

## PROMPT 01 — SEO Blog Post (grantmaker-facing)

**Context:** You are the DR Gives Content + Strategy team. DR Gives is a 501(c)(3)
community wellness nonprofit, the research partner of ECQO Holdings, led by
Bianca D. McCall, LMFT. Our tone is clinically authoritative, trauma-informed,
warm but precise. We never use "DRG" — always "DR Gives" or "Desert Rose Gives".

**Activity:** Write a 1,200-word SEO-optimized blog post on the topic below.
Include: an H1 with the primary keyword, four H2 sections, one H3 under each H2,
two real internal links (/programs, /impact, /financials, or /donate), and a
closing CTA to donate or contact. Include an FAQ block of 3 Q&A at the end
formatted for FAQPage schema.

**Personalization:**
- Primary keyword: [KEYWORD, e.g., "trauma-informed youth mental health Las Vegas"]
- Primary audience: [grantmakers | donors | clinicians | community partners]
- Hook / news angle: [RECENT EVENT or SEASONAL TIE-IN]
- Required citation: [a real outcome from our Impact page — e.g., "70% of
  WellXcel participants reported a reduction in stress symptoms"]

**Output Format:**
1. H1 (include primary keyword)
2. 2-paragraph intro
3. H2 + H3 × 4
4. FAQ block (3 Q&A)
5. CTA paragraph
6. Suggested meta title (60 chars) + meta description (155 chars)

## PROMPT 02 — AEO Q&A page (direct answer to a specific question)

**Context:** You are the DR Gives AEO team. We want ChatGPT, Claude, Perplexity,
and Google's AI Overviews to cite us when users ask a specific question.

**Activity:** Write a single direct-answer page. Begin with a 2-sentence TL;DR
that fully answers the question (for snippet extraction). Then a 600-800 word
body that expands with evidence, citations, and links to our Programs or
Impact page. End with 5 related Q&A pairs for FAQ schema.

**Personalization:**
- Target question: [THE QUESTION, e.g., "How does DR Gives measure WellXcel
  outcomes?"]
- Supporting evidence: [specific number, partner, or quote from our Impact or
  Programs page]

**Output Format:**
1. TL;DR (2 sentences)
2. Full body (600-800 words)
3. "Related questions" Q&A block (5 items)
4. Suggested URL slug
5. Suggested internal links (minimum 2)

## How to use this library efficiently

1. Pick the prompt that matches what you need today.
2. Paste it into Claude.
3. Fill in every **[PERSONALIZATION]** bracket with real, specific data
   (pull from Programs, Impact, or grant documents).
4. Review the output against the Voice & Standards checklist below.
5. Push approved content into the site: blog posts go under `/app/blog/`,
   page updates go directly into the corresponding `/app/[page]/page.tsx`.

## Voice & Standards checklist

Before publishing any AI-generated content, verify every item:

- [ ] Never uses "DRG" — always "DR Gives" or "Desert Rose Gives"
- [ ] Correctly identifies Bianca as LMFT (licensed clinician)
- [ ] Attributes ECQO platform to ECQO Holdings — DR Gives is the research partner
- [ ] Numbers sourced from real outcomes reports, attributed to the specific cycle
- [ ] Trauma-informed language (no sensationalism, no crisis exploitation)
- [ ] Includes 988 where discussing suicide or self-harm risk
- [ ] No medical, legal, or financial advice disguised as marketing
- [ ] FAQ section included for AEO pickup
- [ ] At least two internal links
- [ ] Meta title + description within char limits
- [ ] Primary keyword appears in H1, first paragraph, and one H2
